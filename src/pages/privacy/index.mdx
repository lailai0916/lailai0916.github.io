## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to `https://lailai.one`, its localized versions, and the pages and content on that domain that are directly operated by the site owner (the "Site", "we", "us", or "our").

This Site is a personal content website. It is primarily used to publish notes, blog posts, project information, and related resources. The Site does **not** require account registration for normal browsing and does **not** provide paid purchases or payment processing through the Site itself.

This policy does not automatically apply to:

- separate products or services that may have their own privacy notice, even if they are linked from this Site; or
- third-party websites, apps, repositories, social media platforms, or services that you visit from links on this Site.

## 2. Site Owner and Contact

This Site is operated by the site owner known as `lailai`.

For privacy questions, requests, or concerns, you may contact us at `lailai0x394@gmail.com`.

If you are reporting a security issue or a privacy-rights request, please include a clear subject line such as `Privacy Request` or `Security Report`.

## 3. Information We Collect

We collect only the information that is reasonably necessary to operate, secure, improve, and provide the Site and the features you choose to use.

### 3.1 Information you provide directly

You may provide information directly to us when you:

- contact us by email or through linked communication channels;
- submit comments or reactions through the GitHub Discussions / Giscus commenting feature; or
- otherwise send us information voluntarily.

This may include:

- your name, username, or social handle;
- your email address or other contact details;
- the content of your message, comment, or attachment; and
- any other information you choose to provide.

Please do not send sensitive personal information or confidential materials through public comments or public discussion threads.

### 3.2 Information collected automatically

When you browse or interact with the Site, we and the service providers we use may automatically process technical and usage information such as:

- IP address;
- approximate geolocation derived from IP;
- browser type, browser language, operating system, device type, and screen size;
- referring URL, visited pages, page titles, timestamps, session information, and navigation patterns;
- search queries entered into the Site search feature;
- performance, diagnostic, and error information; and
- network, CDN, and security logs needed to operate and protect the Site.

Some of this information may be considered personal data under applicable law, even when it does not directly identify you by name.

We generally use this information in aggregate or service-operations form and do not try to identify named individuals from ordinary site traffic unless that is reasonably necessary for security, abuse prevention, or troubleshooting.

### 3.3 Information stored in your browser

The Site uses browser storage to remember preferences and support certain interface features. Examples currently include:

| Storage item                                       | Purpose                                                        | Duration           |
| -------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------ |
| `theme`                                            | Remembers light, dark, or system theme preference              | Until you clear it |
| `global-font-size`                                 | Remembers font size preference on the settings page            | Until you clear it |
| `settings-experimental`                            | Remembers optional experimental feature switches               | Until you clear it |
| `ifm-theme-colors-light` / `ifm-theme-colors-dark` | Stores temporary custom color settings for the current session | Session only       |

In addition, third-party services embedded in or loaded by the Site may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies under their own policies.

The Site itself is not designed to use invasive fingerprinting techniques for advertising or cross-site behavioral profiling.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

- provide, operate, and maintain the Site;
- deliver content, search, comments, and related features;
- remember your settings and preferences;
- understand traffic, usage trends, and Site performance;
- troubleshoot errors, protect against abuse, spam, fraud, or security incidents;
- respond to your messages, comments, and requests;
- comply with legal obligations and protect rights, safety, and property; and
- improve the Site's content, structure, and user experience.

We do **not** use information collected through the Site to sell data for money or to run targeted advertising profiles for this Site.

## 9. Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Retention depends on the category of data:

| Category                                    | Retention approach                                                                                     |
| ------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Browser storage and local preferences       | Kept on your device until you clear them, or until the browser or session removes them                 |
| Analytics and security logs                 | Retained for limited periods determined by operational need, provider settings, and legal requirements |
| Search queries and related search telemetry | Retained according to Algolia's settings and policies                                                  |
| Contact emails and correspondence           | Retained as long as needed to respond, keep reasonable records, and protect our rights                 |
| Public comments and reactions               | Remain public until deleted by you, GitHub, or us from the underlying discussion where applicable      |

Because the Site does not maintain a normal end-user account database for browsing, we may not always be able to link anonymous technical or analytics records back to a specific person.
