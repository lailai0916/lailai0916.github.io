## Personal Introduction

I am a **high school student** from Hangzhou, China, currently studying at Hangzhou No.2 High School. My interests center on **mathematics**, **computer science**, and **programming**. My experience in **Olympiad in Informatics** (OI) has shaped my way of thinking and problem-solving.

I learn independently, think systematically, and approach problems through both theory and practice. I value **clarity**, **consistency**, and thoughtful details in code, design, and writing, guided by a design philosophy of **Unity**, **Simplicity**, **Modernity**.

## Website Introduction

This is my personal website for sharing **technical notes**, **project experience**, and **learning insights**. It is built with [Docusaurus](https://docusaurus.io), source code hosted on [GitHub](https://github.com/lailai0916/lailai0916.github.io), and deployed on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com).

For long-form content, you can read competitive programming write-ups in [Contest](/docs/contest), structured study notes in [Note](/docs/note), and end-to-end build logs in [Project](/docs/project), while shorter thoughts and updates live on the [Blog](/blog).

On the more personal side, [Travel](/travel) maps the places I have been, and [Friends](/friends) links to people whose work I admire. The [Resources](/resources) page collects tools and platforms I rely on.

You can fine-tune theme, language, and other preferences on the [Settings](/settings) page, while [Insights](/insights) surfaces real-time site analytics and service health. Follow the iteration history on the [Changelog](/changelog) page, or review how data is handled on the [Privacy Policy](/privacy) page.
